STEAK FRITES

Difficulty: Easy

Steak and french fries with sauce

  • Steak
  • Fries
  • White Wine
  • Heavy Cream
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Chives
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  1. Season steak after letting rest at room temperature
  2. Fill a pot halfway with oil to deep fry frozen fries (or fresh if preferred)
  3. Cook steak to preferred doneness
  4. Place fries in oil pot, occasionally stirring to prevent fries from sticking to eachother or pot
  5. Remove steak from frying pan and let rest
  6. In the same frying pan the steak was in, pour in white wine to deglaze the pan
  7. Add heavy cream and dijon mustard, adjust according to taste
  8. Once sauce is at preferred taste levels and has thickened up a bit, add chopped chives
  9. When fries are finished, remove from pot and place on paper towel to remove excess oil
  10. After steak has thoroughly rested (at least 15 minutes) cut into slices to plate
  11. Plate the slices of steak alongside fries and generously drizzle over steak (enough sauce to dip fries in)
