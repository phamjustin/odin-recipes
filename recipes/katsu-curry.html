CHICKEN KATSU CURRY

Difficulty: Medium

Battered chicken breast in curry over rice

  • Chicken Breast
  • Curry Cubes
  • Green Onion
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Pickled Ginger (Optional)
  1. After flattening the chicken breast, dip in egg, flour, and breadcrumb in that order
  2. Shallow fry breast on both sides
  3. Place on wire rack when finished to remove excess oil
  4. Prepare the curry according to box instructions
  5. If took thick add water, if too thin add potato starch
  6. Add chopped vegatables into curry pot
  7. Plate chicken on top of curry with rice
