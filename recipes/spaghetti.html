SPAGHETTI

Difficulty: Easy

Mom's Spaghetti

  • Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Chopped White Onion
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Flakes
  • Fish Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • MSG
  • Ragu Pasta Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  1. Boil pasta according to box directions
  2. Sautee garlic and onions
  3. Add ground beef and cook well
  4. Add salt, pepper, sugar, fish sauce, garlic powder, msg, chili flakes, and Mushrooms
  5. Add ragu pasta sauce, add more sugar and fish according to taste
